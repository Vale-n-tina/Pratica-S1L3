Focaccia whit Caramelized onion,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow yours taste bud whit this festive focaccia appetizer tepped whit sweet caramelizet onion, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoones instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoones salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cupsextra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes ( scrape rind off whit a paring knife if desired, thougt it's not
    necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer whit paddle attachment, mix together all
the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the
dough, though i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorrporate
the last of the flour...
